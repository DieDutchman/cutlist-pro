## Priority Issues

### [P2] Material override has no revert path

Unit quantities revert per-section (↩). But material overrides on units have no recovery. A wrong material silently changes cost calculations with no undo path.

**Fix:** Same pattern as _sectionQtySnapshots but for unitMatOverrides keyed by sectionId. Snapshot at section render; show ↩ when any override differs; revert restores overrides for that section's units.

**Suggested command:** /impeccable craft unit material override revert

---

### [P2] No batch panel material change

Changing material for 20 panels requires 20 individual selects. The highest-friction workflow for high-volume users.

**Fix:** Add a "Change material for all panels" select above the panel table, or a multi-select + bulk-apply mode.

**Suggested command:** /impeccable craft batch panel material

---

### [P3] Excel green hover state unregistered

#217a4b (darker Excel green hover, line 155) not in DESIGN.md. excel-green: "#1d6f42" is registered but its hover variant isn't.

**Fix:** Add excel-green-dark: "#217a4b" to DESIGN.md colors block.
